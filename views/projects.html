Prodicting Chladni Patterns using Fourier Analysis

This project investigates the vibrational modes of a thin plate utilizing the biharmonic partial differential equation. Theoretical solutions are derived to predict the formation of Chladni patterns as the plate is driven at various frequencies by a mechanical wave driver.
